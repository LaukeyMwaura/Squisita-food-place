Squisita Pizza Place

The place

A Squisita pizza box might be one of the most recognisable icons of Africa.



Whichever way you slice it, Squisita Pizza Place is an industry leader in the quick service pizza game.

The 1st Squisita outlet opened its doors in 1994 in Harare,

Zimbabwe, and rose quickly in popularity.

Serving hot oven-fresh pizza with its signature all-natural tomato sauce and using only the freshest ingredients, Squisita has established itself at the top of the industry and is easily one of the first choices in quick service pizza in Africa.
